üí° How Question Pools Work

  • Endless Mode: Uses assigned questions for survival gameplay. Students face random questions until they run out of health.
  • Test Yourself: Creates 40-question assessments from assigned questions. You can control difficulty distribution and time limits.
  • Level-Based: Traditional progression through levels 1-10. Questions are organized by difficulty and topic.

üí° Tip: You can assign the same questions to multiple pools, or create completely separate question sets for different game modes!
